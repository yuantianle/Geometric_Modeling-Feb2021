Keyboard guidance: 
1=> Original shape with tensor field
2=>	Regular subdivision
3=>	Irregular subdivision
4=> Smooth
5=> Heat diffusion
6=> Original shape with white color
7=> Shape with 3D checkerboard color 
or 3D checkerboard coordinates texture
9=> Silhouette
P=> Tensor Smoothing

s=> Swap smooth and flat
t=> Triangle mesh

Deformation functions:

After calculating the rotated displacement of soft constrain points 
and setting it manually in the headfile named "RIdeformation.h":

Click middle button of mouse to choose a triangle mesh, a pair of 
clicking will decide an anchor point.

c=> Clear all the anchor points selections

Click right button of mouse to deform the mesh. (the default 
mesh is the mesh of Cuboid model)
